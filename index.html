Instant messaging (IM) has been around for over a decade, so it is more than technology of the present. It is a record of our past, stored on sites like Facebook and Google. This is a particularly interesting technology because it is written communication with no time delay, which has not existed before in human history.

By analyzing the IM conversations a person has had throughout their digital life, one can build a statistical model of a relationship online. This is useful to digital anthropologists and other researchers as they can gain a deeper understanding of how friendships are maintained online.

Chatlytics will give researchers and curious social media users the tools to perform such an analysis of this new communication form without costly and complex software.

Facebook Chat statistics

After you import your exported Facebook chat history into our software, you get access to a variety of metrics on how you interact online. Chat activity per person, average reply time, changes in activity, and more.

Understand how you communicate

With your chat history our software can help you understand what you commonly say, and even come up with a sentence we think you would have said! The data also allows for more traditional analysis such as word and phrase counts. Other statistics include chat activity per day.

We respect your privacy

We recognize that chat history is very sensitive, so we want to reassure you that it is never leaves the safety of your own computer. You download our application instead of going to some website and uploading your chat history. We will never collect information about you and store it in the cloud or anywhere else on the Internet. Your personal data remains in your control.

When the beta is launched, we will make the code open-source so anyone can inspect it to their heart's content.
